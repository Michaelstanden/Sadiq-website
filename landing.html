ABOUT

A mixed heritage circus artist from Scotland - Sadiq has been entertaining and wowing on stages all over. Most recently holding residency at London’s famous Café De Paris and touring with the UK’s leading contemporary circus company Ockham’s Razor – you’ll find him up and down all manner of poles (Chines Pole, Free Standing and Aerial). In these pages you will see a mix of his acts, his theatrical circus works The Chosen Haram and a range of modelling – from Editorial to Fetish. Contact Sadiq if for anymore information, requests or to make booking!
